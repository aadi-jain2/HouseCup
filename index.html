Point System Guide

Core Tiers for All Activities

Participation
1-3 points
Basic involvement without awards or leadership
School Level
5 points
Achievement or leadership recognized at school level
Regional/State
10 points
Participation or achievement at regional or state level
National Level
20 points
Participation or achievement at the national level
National Award
30 points
Winner or recipient of a national-level award
Special Mention
25 points
Jury special mention or unique recognition

Academic Activities

Participation (1 pt): Member of academic clubs, volunteer in projects
School Level (5 pts): Leadership in academic clubs, school science fair winner
Regional/State (10 pts): State Olympiad finalist, regional debate wins
National (20 pts): National Olympiad winner/finalist, national debate
National Award (30 pts): National academic award winner, international Olympiad top rank

Sports Activities

Participation (1 pt): Team member without distinction
School Level (5 pts): Team captain, school tournament winner
Regional/State (10 pts): State championships or equivalent
National (20 pts): National level tournaments or leagues
National Award (30 pts): National champion, MVP, or special recognition

Arts and Creative Activities

Participation (1 pt): Member of choir, art club participation
School Level (5 pts): Lead role in school play, school art show awards
Regional/State (10 pts): State-level arts competition awards
National (20 pts): National art/music competitions, major performances
National Award (30 pts): National awards in arts, performances at prestigious events

Leadership and Community Service

Participation (1 pt): Volunteer without significant project roles
School Level (5 pts): Club officer, team captain, community service leader
Regional/State (10 pts): Leading regional community initiative or larger volunteer roles
National (20 pts): Founder of recognized national service project, major awards
National Award (30 pts): National service award, significant public recognition
